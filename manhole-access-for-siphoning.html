Booking guide · Practical guide

Many homes have lids hidden by soil, planters, tiles, or storage. Mentioning that early is better than discovering it only when the truck is already expected.

Look for known markers first

Check old service notes, ask previous occupants, or look for circular covers in common yard or driveway locations. Do not force covers open.

Send a safe photo

A clear photo from standing height helps more than a close shot taken while prying at a lid. Include nearby walls or gates for scale.

Describe distance and obstacles

Note fences, parked cars, garden beds, stairs, or long hose runs from the street to the access point.

Flag covered or uncertain lids

If you suspect the opening is under tiles or soil, say so. Access clarification belongs in the first conversation.

Keep safety first

Never enter a tank space. Keep children away from open lids. Leave uncovering and handling to the service team when needed.
