# SEO + Content Plan

## Positioning
Jhapher Malabanan is a local septic and declogging service centered in Pasig.

Voice: conversational Taglish, problem-led, direct, reassuring.

## Avoid
- guaranteed ranking claims
- fake branch/location claims
- fake reviews
- `#1` or `best` claims unless proven
- thin copied city pages
- 24/7 claims unless confirmed

## Site architecture
1. `/` Home
2. `/services` Services hub
3. `/services/septic-tank-siphoning`
4. `/services/pozo-negro-cleaning`
5. `/services/declogging`
6. `/services/emergency-malabanan-service` only if urgent/same-day wording is confirmed
7. `/service-areas`
8. Priority location pages later, not first

## Homepage SEO
Title:
`Jhapher Malabanan Services | Septic Siphoning, Pozo Negro Cleaning & Declogging`

Meta:
`Need septic siphoning, pozo negro cleaning, or declogging? Jhapher Malabanan serves Pasig, Metro Manila, Rizal, and nearby areas. Message or call for a clear estimate.`

## Priority location rollout
Build strong hub first. Then add pages in this order:
1. Pasig
2. Cainta
3. Taytay
4. Antipolo
5. Marikina
6. Taguig
7. Mandaluyong
8. Quezon City

## Customer problem copy angles
- `Barado ang CR?`
- `Mabagal bumaba ang tubig?`
- `Mabaho ang drain?`
- `Puno na septic tank?`
- `Hindi sure kung siphoning or declogging ang kailangan?`

## FAQ topics
- Magkano magpa-siphon ng septic tank?
- Available ba same-day declogging?
- Anong areas ang covered?
- Ano ang kailangan i-send before estimate?
- Siphoning ba or declogging ang kailangan?

## Content model for GitHub/Vercel
Use structured content so pages stay consistent:
- services data
- service area data
- FAQ data
- contact data
- SEO metadata data

## SEO principle
Clear structure first. City pages later only when each page has useful unique content.
