## International/local-service best-practice pattern
Strong septic/plumbing/drain websites usually have:
- urgent problem-led hero
- phone CTA above the fold
- service cards with customer symptoms
- service-area hub
- trust/proof section
- simple booking process
- FAQs
- LocalBusiness/Service schema
- favicon/logo
- sitemap and robots
- fast mobile layout

## Strategic direction for Jhapher
The website should feel like:

`May urgent septic or bara problem? Here is the service, here is the area, here is how to call, here is what to send.`

Not like:
- agency portfolio
- overdesigned landing page
- fake corporate brand
- SEO keyword dump
