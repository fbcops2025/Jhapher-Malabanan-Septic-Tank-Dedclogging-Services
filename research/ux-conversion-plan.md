# UX + Conversion Plan

## Main goal
Make urgent customers understand fast:

**Yes, Jhapher does the service. Yes, he covers the area. Yes, this is how to contact him now.**

## Core pages
1. Home
2. Services
3. Service Areas
4. About / Why Choose Us
5. Contact
6. FAQ

## Homepage flow
1. Hero
   - Headline: `Baradong CR? Puno na septic tank? Tawag agad sa Jhapher Malabanan.`
   - Subheadline: `Septic tank siphoning, pozo negro cleaning, at declogging services. Main center sa Pasig City, serving NCR and nearby provinces.`
   - CTA: call + Viber

2. Problem-based service cards
   - Puno na septic tank?
   - Barado ang CR o lababo?
   - Mabaho o umaapaw ang pozo negro?

3. Why customers call
   - Pasig City center area
   - NCR and nearby coverage
   - Direct call / Viber contact
   - Residential and commercial service

4. Service areas
   - Show Pasig first
   - Group NCR, Rizal, nearby provinces, and extended areas

5. How it works
   - Tumawag o mag-Viber
   - I-send ang location at problem
   - Confirm schedule
   - Team handles service

6. Proof section
   - Use real crew, truck, equipment, before/after, customer screenshot, or Facebook proof only if approved

7. Final CTA
   - `Huwag nang hintayin umapaw o lumala ang bara. Tawag na sa Jhapher Malabanan.`

## CTA strategy
Primary: Call Now  
Secondary: Viber Message  
Mobile: sticky bottom CTA with Call + Viber

## Design direction
- clean local service website
- practical, trustworthy, not too corporate
- strong mobile CTA
- navy / dark green base
- orange or yellow CTA accent

## Caution
Confirm Cagayan de Oro and Misamis Occidental before heavily featuring them. They are far from Pasig and may imply separate branches or teams.
