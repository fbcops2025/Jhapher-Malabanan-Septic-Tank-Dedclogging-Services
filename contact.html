Before you call

Frequently asked questions

How is an estimate determined?

Location, likely service, tank or blockage conditions, access, property type, and actual work scope can affect the estimate. Share details first for a more useful assessment.

Can service be scheduled on the same day?

Availability depends on team schedule, location, job type, and access. Call first when the problem is urgent.

What if I do not know whether I need siphoning or declogging?

Describe which fixtures are affected and whether there is odor, gurgling, overflow, or wastewater return. The team can suggest the practical next step.

Is my area covered?

Pasig is the center area. Metro Manila, Rizal, Cavite, Laguna, Bulacan, and selected extended locations are handled by confirmation.
